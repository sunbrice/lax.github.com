--- layout: post name: google-sets-is-cool title: Google Sets is cool! time: 2009-08-25 14:21:00.000000000 +08:00 --- Use Google Sets to expand a list of a few items, into a list with many items. For example let's suppose you want a set (or list) of Star Wars characters, but can only think of a few. Enter a the ones you can think of, and Google sets will return a whole list.

links: http://labs.google.com/sets
